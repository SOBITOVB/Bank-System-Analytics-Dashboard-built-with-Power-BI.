Bank Power BI Dashboard Roadmap
1. Data Preparation and Modeling
Import Data:
Load CSV fayllar:

Dim_Date.csv

Dim_Region.csv 

Dim_Customers.csv

Dim_Branches.csv

Dim_Products.csv

Dim_Channel.csv 

Dim_Loans.csv

Fact_Transactions.csv

Fact_LoanPayments.csv 

Clean Data:
Qo‘shimcha jadvallarni tekshirish:

Dim_Region: Region_ID, Region_Name_Uz, Region_Name_Eng (matn)

Dim_Channel: Channel_ID, Channel_Name, Channel_Type

Fact_LoanPayments: Payment_ID, Loan_ID, Payment_Date, Payment_Amount, Principal, Interest (o‘nlik). Bo‘sh qiymatlar yo‘q.

Build Data Model:
Munosabatlar (1:*):

Fact_Transactions[Date_Key] → Dim_Date[Date_Key]

Fact_Transactions[Customer_ID] → Dim_Customers[Customer_ID]

Fact_Transactions[Branch_ID] → Dim_Branches[Branch_ID]

Fact_Transactions[Product_ID] → Dim_Products[Product_ID]

Fact_Transactions[Channel_ID] → Dim_Channel[Channel_ID]

Dim_Loans[Customer_ID] → Dim_Customers[Customer_ID]

Dim_Branches[Region_ID] → Dim_Region[Region_ID]

Dim_Customers[Region_ID] → Dim_Region[Region_ID]

Fact_LoanPayments[Loan_ID] → Dim_Loans[Loan_ID]

Dim_Date ustunlari: Year, Quarter, Month_Name, Is_Weekend.
Dim_Branches dagi Latitude/Longitude – xarita vizualizatsiyasi.
Dim_Region dagi Region_Name_Eng – Shape Map uchun location maydoni.

2. Define KPIs (qo‘shimchalar)
Kanaldan foydalanish ulushi:
Mobile Usage % = DIVIDE(CALCULATE(COUNTROWS(Fact_Transactions), Dim_Channel[Channel_Name]="Mobile App"), COUNTROWS(Fact_Transactions))
(Shunga o‘xshash Web Usage %, ATM Usage %)

O‘rtacha oylik to‘lov (kredit bo‘yicha):
Avg Monthly Payment = AVERAGEX(Fact_LoanPayments, Fact_LoanPayments[Payment_Amount])

To‘lov intizomi (%):
Payment Discipline = DIVIDE( COUNTROWS(Fact_LoanPayments), COUNTROWS(SUMMARIZE(Fact_LoanPayments, Fact_LoanPayments[Loan_ID])) * (maksimal to‘lovlar soni) )
Soddaroq: Discipline Rate = 1 - (COUNTROWS(FILTER(Fact_LoanPayments, Fact_LoanPayments[Payment_Date] > RELATED(Dim_Loans[Due_Date])))) (Agar due_date mavjud bo‘lsa; hozircha mavjud emas – keyingi versiyaga).

Qolgan qarz dinamikasi (Fact_LoanPayments orqali):
Remaining Debt Trend = SUM(Dim_Loans[Remaining_Debt]) – vaqt bo‘yicha.

3. Design Visualizations
Sahifa 1: Executive Dashboard
(o‘zgarishsiz)

Sahifa 2: Mijozlar tahlili

Xarita (Shape Map): Region_Name_Eng asosida, rang – Total Transaction Value yoki NPL Ratio.

Stacked bar chart: Gender va Age.

Jadval: Top 10 mijozlar.

Sahifa 3: Filiallar va hududlar

Bubble xarita (Latitude/Longitude).

Shtrixli diagramma: Plan Achievement.

Matritsa: Branch_Name, City, xatolik ulushi.

Sahifa 4: Kredit portfeli

Donut: NPL ulushi.

Gistogramma: kredit summalari.

Vaqt seriyasi: Qolgan qarz va to‘lovlar dinamikasi (Line chart – Remaining_Debt va Payment_Amount oylar bo‘yicha).

Yangi: To‘lov intizomi gauge (target 95%).

Sahifa 5: Operatsion samaradorlik

Pie/Donut chart: Dim_Channel[Channel_Name] bo‘yicha tranzaksiyalar soni.

Bar chart: Xatoliklar Product_Name bo‘yicha.

Karta: Kutilayotgan tranzaksiyalar soni.

Stacked column chart: Kanallar bo‘yicha muvaffaqiyat darajasi.

Design Tips (o‘zgarishsiz, faqat Product_Category va Channel slicer qo‘shiladi)

4. Add Interactivity
Slicerlarga qo‘shimcha:

Channel_Name (Mobile, Web, ATM, Filial, Call Center)

Region_Name_Uz (yoki Eng)

Product_Category

Drill-down:

Vaqt: Year → Quarter → Month_Name

Mahsulot: Category → Product_Name

Hudud: Region_Name_Uz → City (filial shaharlari)

Cross-filtering va Bookmarks – o‘zgarishsiz.

5. Derive Insights
Qo‘shimcha insaytlar:

Kanallar samaradorligi: Qaysi kanal eng ko‘p muvaffaqiyatli tranzaksiyalarga ega? Qaysi kanalda xatolik ko‘p?

To‘lov intizomi: Qaysi hudud yoki kasb vakillari kreditni o‘z vaqtida to‘laydi?

Kredit to‘lovlari vs yangi kreditlar: To‘lovlar va yangi kreditlar o‘rtasidagi bog‘liqlik.

6. Testing and Validation
Yangi jadvallar uchun tekshiruv:

Fact_LoanPayments dagi Loan_ID larning barchasi Dim_Loans da mavjudmi?

Fact_Transactions[Channel_ID] Dim_Channel ga bog‘langanmi?

Shape Map uchun Region_Name_Eng uz.json nomlariga to‘liq mos keladimi (Tashkent, Samarkand, Ferghana, Bukhoro, Andijon, Namangan, Kashkadarya, Surkhandarya, Khorezm, Navoi, Jizzakh, Sirdaryo, Karakalpakstan).

KPI hisoblari (masalan, kanal ulushlari yig‘indisi 100% bo‘lishi kerak).

7. Deployment and Sharing
Publish: .pbix faylni Power BI Service ga yuklang.

Refresh:

Ma’lumotlar har kuni yangilanadigan bo‘lsa, Gateway orqali avtomatik yangilanishni sozlang (yoki CSV larni OneDrive/SharePoint ga joylashtiring).

Share:

Power BI ilovasini yaratib, tegishli foydalanuvchilarga ruxsat bering.

Agar kerak bo‘lsa, Excel yoki PDF eksport qilish imkoniyatini qoldiring.

8. Maintenance and Iteration
Monitor: Power BI Service’da “Usage metrics” orqali dashboard qanchalik ishlatilayotganini kuzating.

Update:

Yangi KPI qo‘shish (masalan, “Fraud tranzaksiyalar ulushi” – agar ma’lumot mavjud bo‘lsa).

Vaqt o‘tishi bilan Dim_Date ni yangilash (2025+ yillar uchun).

Refine:

Foydalanuvchi mulohazalariga asosan vizuallar, ranglar yoki sahifalar tartibini o‘zgartiring.

Eng sekin vizuallarni optimallashtiring (masalan, DISTINCTCOUNT o‘rniga COUNTROWS + VALUES).
